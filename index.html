About

Who We Are

INSUKOON is a start-up company formed with the core purpose of accelerating imperative growth of every child through a very well-researched curriculum. Our program emphasizes the importance of non-academic subjects for students, teachers, parents and school management which we firmly believe is ‘Future of Education’.

At INSUKOON we strongly perceive that each step taken by a child through our well-designed curriculum will be one giant leap towards a better mankind.

To Learn more, please watch the video: Play Video   

Our Mission

To tap into the intrinsic motivation of every child in the world by reshaping their days towards leading a phenomenal life.

Our Vision

To make INSUKOON a lifetime experience by breathing hope to make dreams into reality.

Success Mantra

"Empowering, Not Educating"
Education promotes individual growth but empowerment boosts growth of the community.

Unique Curriculum

Female Workforce

“The benefits of women workforce speak for themselves.” We believe in sweetening the pot with greater pool of talent, creativity, innovation, dedication and especially patience when it comes to dealing and connecting with kids. We decided to have female employees onboard with a belief that females are more nurturing and are ideal candidate to fill the need of the hour.
